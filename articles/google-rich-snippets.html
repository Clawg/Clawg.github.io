Google Rich Snippets – Review and Events

I have added event and review rich snippets to the venue pages and business pages on STVLocal. Rich snippets allow you to tag information within the page which Google can then take and use as part of the Search Engine Results Page. For a venue Google will pull out 3 events for that venue, giving the user detail of the event name, a link to the event, event start time and event address.

For businesses Google can show the businesses average rating, who last reviewed the business and average price for a meal.

Google does not guarantee that they will display this information, however if you don’t by a ticket you can’t win the lottery. If anyone ever sees any of the STVLocal pages in Google displaying the snippet information then please let me know.

  • You can find more about rich snippets on the Google webmasters blog
  • You can test any page for rich snippets using Google snippet testing tool, try putting in any of STVLocals venue or business page URL nto the form
